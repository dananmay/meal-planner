M1: Soya Chunk Bhuna with Jeera Rice
Calories: 460 kcal | Protein: 34g | Carbs: 52g | Fat: 12g
Prep time: 30 min | Heat: 🌶️🌶️ | Tags: high-protein, comfort-food, rice-based
Ingredients:
* Soya chunks: 50g
* Basmati rice: 60g
* Tomato: 100g
* Ginger: 5g
* Green chili: 10g
* Cumin seeds: 1 tsp
* Asafoetida: ¼ tsp
* Turmeric: ½ tsp
* Coriander powder: 1 tsp
* Garam masala: ½ tsp
* Kashmiri red chili powder: 1 tsp
* Mustard oil: 8g
* Fresh coriander: 10g
* Salt: to taste
Instructions:
1. Soak soya chunks in hot water for 15 min, squeeze out excess water, and marinate with ½ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp Kashmiri chili powder, and a pinch of salt for 10 min.
2. Cook basmati rice with cumin seeds dry-roasted in the pot first — use 60g dry rice to about 130ml water.
3. Heat mustard oil in a kadhai. Add cumin seeds and hing, let splutter.
4. Add ginger, green chili, sauté 1 min.
5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder. Cook until oil separates, about 5 min.
6. Add marinated soya chunks. Stir well, cook on medium heat for 8 min.
7. Finish with garam masala and fresh coriander.
8. Serve soya bhuna alongside jeera rice.

M4: Tamarind Dal Fry with Roti
